Serving

3 to 4 servings

Cooking duration

30 to 40 min

Calories

250-300 calories

Nutritional Information

  • Calories: Approx. 250-300
  • Protein: Around 4-6g
  • Fiber: 1-2g
  • Carbohydrates: 40-45g
  • Fat: 8-10g

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice (washed and soaked)
  • 1/2 cup fresh mint leaves (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup fresh coriander leaves (chopped)
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2-3 green chilies (finely chopped)
  • 1-inch ginger (finely grated)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2-3 cloves
  • 2-3 tbsp cooking oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste

Preperation Method

  1. Prepare the Rice:Wash and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. Mint Paste:Blend mint leaves, coriander leaves, chilies, ginger, and garlic into a smooth paste.
  3. Cooking:Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin, mustard seeds, cinnamon, and cloves. Sauté.Add onions, sauté till translucent. Add mint paste, cook until aroma develops.Mix in partially cooked rice. Cook on low heat until fully done.
  4. Serve:Garnish with fresh mint and coriander leaves.
