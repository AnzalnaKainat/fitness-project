Pull Ups

How To Do

  1. Grip: Start by gripping the pull-up bar with your palms facing away from you, slightly wider than shoulder-width apart.
  2. Hang: Hang from the bar with your arms fully extended. Engage your core and keep your body straight.
  3. Pull: Pull yourself up by engaging your back muscles, bending your elbows, and bringing your chest towards the bar.
  4. Chin Over Bar: Continue pulling until your chin clears the bar, and your chest is close to it.
  5. Lower: Slowly lower yourself back down to the starting position, extending your arms fully but maintaining control.
  6. Repeat: Aim for multiple repetitions while maintaining proper form.
